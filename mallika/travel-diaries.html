2025

June: Visakhapatnam & Puri

Coastal adventures in Vizag and the holy city of Puri in Odisha, with its temples and beaches.

2024

March: Silvassa

Exploring the union territory of Dadra and Nagar Haveli, with its tribal culture and natural beauty.

Kumbhalgarh Fort, a 15th-century UNESCO World Heritage Site in Rajasthan, is famed for its massive 38-km-long wall—the second longest in the world after the Great Wall of China.

November: Warangal & Karimnagar

Historical Telangana cities, Warangal with its thousand pillar temple and Karimnagar's heritage sites.

April: Dwarka

Pilgrimage to Dwarka, the ancient city of Lord Krishna, with its temples and coastal charm.

October: Visakhapatnam

Another visit to Vizag, perhaps discovering new spots or revisiting favorites.

December: Alibaug

Beach holiday in Alibaug, enjoying the Konkan coastline, forts, and fresh seafood.

August: Udaipur

The city of lakes, Udaipur, with its romantic palaces, lakes, and the fairy-tale ambiance of Rajasthan.

Bustling Mumbai with its vibrant energy, followed by a return to Hyderabad for more explorations.

Spiritual journey to Ujjain for the Kumbh Mela vibes and exploring the cultural hub of Indore.

June: Rani ki Vav

The City of Pearls with iconic landmarks like Charminar, Golconda Fort, and world-famous Hyderabadi biryani.
