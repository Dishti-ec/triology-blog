Solaris Nexus

An experimental playground for generating, harnessing, and storing solar energy.

Overview

Solaris Nexus is an exploratory project focused on crafting small interactive experiences using HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript. This lab captures ideas around starfield visuals, cursor micro-interactions, and responsive layout experiments. The goal is to iterate quickly, document learnings, and surface design decisions that could be reused across other projects.

Goals

  • Create a lightweight starfield visual system
  • Design micro-interactions that feel responsive and pleasant
  • Keep performance and accessibility in mind

Progress Notes

The initial prototype includes a header with a starry background and a cursor-follow sparkle effect. Experiment files, code snippets, and screenshots will be added here as the lab evolves.
