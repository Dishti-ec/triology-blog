Books I’d Recommend Without Thinking Twice

The ones that stayed longer than expected.

These are the books I gift, lend, and return to. They change the way I read and live.

Some of them arrived at difficult times; others stayed because I kept finding new passages to underline. They are companions rather than trophies.

If a book asked me to have fewer answers and more questions, it's likely on this list — the ones I hand to friends without hesitation.
