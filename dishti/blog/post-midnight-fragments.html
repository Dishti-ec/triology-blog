Midnight Fragments

Half sentences worth keeping.

Focus is finite. Boredom is data. Deadlines turn sketches into files.

This is a small list of beliefs I return to when a side project starts eating the week.

Late nights teach a certain economy of attention — pare down the noise, keep the line that matters, and ship. These fragments are anchors I come back to when focus drifts.

Some nights I write only to remember the shape of an idea; other nights I write to finish it. Both feel necessary.
