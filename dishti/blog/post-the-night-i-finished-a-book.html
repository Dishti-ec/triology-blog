The Night I Finished a Book in One Sitting

When a story refuses to let you sleep.

There are nights when a book becomes a small companion that won't let you go. The hours blur, and the ending arrives like a quiet reward.

That feeling of being carried by a story is both rare and generous; it reshapes how you think the next morning. I keep a list of those books for when I need that restless companionship again.

Reading like this reminds me that attention can be a pilgrimage — we follow a story until it chooses to give us something back.
