Songs That Feel Like Breathing

The kind of music you don’t just hear—you sit with.

Some songs become part of how you move through the day. They mark moods, seasons, and small transformations.

There are tracks that arrive and settle, like a memory you can step into. I keep a small list of songs that open the same space each time I play them.

Music has taught me how to notice transitions — from busy to quiet, from work to stillness — and to honor those pauses.
