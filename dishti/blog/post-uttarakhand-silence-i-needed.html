Uttarakhand, and the Silence I Needed

Mountains, distance, and thoughts that finally made sense.

The mountains had a way of rearranging the inside of my head. The quiet there was not empty; it was a space where thinking could finally breathe.

I spent long mornings tracing ridgelines and long evenings learning how to be present with less. The landscape taught me how patience changes perception.

Sometimes the best thinking comes when you stop trying to make it happen and let the place do its work.

Thoughts don’t end here.
