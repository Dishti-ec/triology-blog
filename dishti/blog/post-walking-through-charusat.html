Walking Through CHARUSAT

A visit that made the future feel a little more real.

Sometimes a single campus tour can make abstract plans feel tangible. That walk through gates and labs nudged the future a little closer.

I remember pausing under a banyan tree and imagining projects sketched across the lawn. The place made ideas feel like invitations rather than obligations.

The visit left me with a quiet confidence — the sense that places can shape the work we choose to do.
